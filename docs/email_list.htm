WRF-Chem email list

A new email group for the WRF-Chem user community:

https://list.woc.noaa.gov/cgi-bin/mailman/listinfo/wrf-chem-discussions

The objectives of this email list (WRF-Chem discussions) are to reach out to the large worldwide WRF-Chem users community for important announcements and updates, to facilitate exchange of ideas and ongoing model developments among the users, and to foster collaboration among different research groups.

All members of the email list will be able to send emails to the entire WRF-Chem discussions group. However, all the email need to be approved by moderators before sending out to the entire group in order to eliminate spam.

For any email list questions, please feel free to contact us via wrfchemhelp.gsd@noaa.gov

Questions should be directed to WRF-Chem Support.
