Tips for getting help faster

  • Include your WRF and WRF-Chem version, compiler, and platform.
  • Paste the exact error message and the lines before it.
  • Attach or quote the relevant namelist sections.
  • If the issue is runtime, include the last 100 to 200 lines of rsl.* output.
